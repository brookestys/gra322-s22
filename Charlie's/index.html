Charlie's Cafe

About

Charlie's Cafe, one of Sun Devil Dining's local restaurant partners, brings the taste of Europe to ASU! Proudly serving Firecreek Coffee and featuring fresh European sandwiches and baked goods. Come experience the difference! With three locations on campus, there is always a Charlie's Cafe near! Charlie's Cafe is located at the Hayden Library, Bio Design B Building and the College of Design.
